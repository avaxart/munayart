Avax Punk by Munay Art are here!

The collection is designed to develop the culture of Avalanche network. All the NFTs are dedicated to Avax ecosystem and are hand-drawn.

Why Avalanche?

Avalanche is an open, programmable smart contracts platform for decentralized applications. Mint your own digital collectibles in seconds for fees less than a cent. Digitally prove ownership, and streamline value flow. Create and share art, collectibles, and more




What does Avax Punk mean?

After discovering the wonderful crypto world and "A Cypherpunk's Manifesto" I have decided to dedicate my life to supporting this new system that is being born, the blockchain is a technological revolution and cryptocurrencies are the next social and cultural revolution that will change the way of seeing the world as we know it! Avax Punk is a collection of drawings that has a rebellious influence. With these drawings I want to portray the Avax ecosystem and everything that happens around it.
